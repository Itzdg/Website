FAQs

How do I know if my order was placed successfully?

You will recieve a confirmation email within 24 hours of placing your order telling you that your order has been placed successfully.

How long will it take for my order to be delivered?

You can view the estimated delivery time by going to the orders page.

How can I modify my order?

To modify your order, contact us via email at orders@caesd.shop before your order has been shipped.

Do you offer free international shipping?

Yes, we offer free international shipping on orders over HK$300 (US$38).
